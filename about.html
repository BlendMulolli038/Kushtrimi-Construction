Our Story

Building excellence since 2008

Kushtrimi Construction was founded with a vision to transform the construction industry through innovative design, quality craftsmanship, and unparalleled customer service. What began as a small family business has grown into one of the region's most respected construction firms.

Over the years, we've built a reputation for delivering projects that combine aesthetic appeal with functional design, always prioritizing our clients' needs and visions. Our portfolio includes everything from luxury residences to commercial complexes, each project reflecting our commitment to excellence.

Our Journey

Milestones in our growth and development

2008

Founded as a small residential construction company with just 5 employees, completing our first home renovation project in Prishtina.

2012

Expanded into commercial construction with our first office building project. Hired our first in-house architect to offer design-build services.

2015

Opened our second location in Tirana and completed our 100th project. Received "Builder of the Year" award from Kosovo Construction Association.

2018

Launched our sustainable building division, completing our first LEED-certified project. Expanded team to over 50 skilled professionals.

2021

Celebrated our 500th completed project. Expanded services to include full interior design and 3D visualization capabilities.

2023

Opened our new headquarters in Prishtina with state-of-the-art design center. Recognized as one of the fastest-growing construction firms in the region.
