Arta Krasniqi

Meet Our Architect

Arta Krasniqi brings over 12 years of architectural experience to Kushtrimi Construction. With a Master's degree in Architecture from the University of Prishtina and additional training in sustainable design from Milan, Arta leads our design team with creativity and technical expertise.

Her portfolio includes award-winning residential projects, innovative commercial spaces, and sensitive heritage renovations. Arta's approach combines aesthetic vision with practical functionality, ensuring spaces that are beautiful, livable, and sustainable.
